{% raw %}By this time we had reached the State of Louisiana. I saw the sign of a den¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tist, and naturally being interested I looked closer and found that F. D. Henry{% endraw %}
{% raw %}had been spending his time since he graduated crowning "shine teeth" for seventy-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}five cents per. As it was time to partake of our daily repast, we descended and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}found to our surprise that we were in Cheneyville. Remembering that our dear{% endraw %}
{% raw %}old friend, Rutledge, was from that metropolis, we immediately inquired for him.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The gentleman to whom we referred our inquiry, looked on us with some disgust{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and asked us if it was necessary for him to have to direct us to the biggest "ladies'{% endraw %}
{% raw %}man" in the State of Louisiana. Bang! came our memory back to us, for was it{% endraw %}
{% raw %}not C. E. Rutledge who put Spring Street on the map of Atlanta ? We saw him{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to talk to and we saw him to listen to. We found, however, that he had a close{% endraw %}
{% raw %}second in the person of R. C. Murphy, and oh my! that baritone voice of Murphy's{% endraw %}
{% raw %}was just so captivating that he would sit on a fence and sing and the girls would{% endraw %}
{% raw %}come to him like hunting dogs answering a horn. We had to leave hurriedly.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}We got over into Georgia and hit Savannah. Here we tried to land. Bob{% endraw %}
{% raw %}"cut off" too soon and we landed much quicker than anticipated. Some one called{% endraw %}
{% raw %}for an ambulance and when it came who should be driving it but Armona. He{% endraw %}
{% raw %}simply worked there to be with the nurses. On the way to the hospital a "skirt"{% endraw %}
{% raw %}waved at him and he stopped to talk to her, but we couldn't stand that, and after{% endraw %}
{% raw %}much persuasion we succeeded in getting him to take us on. (We don't know{% endraw %}
{% raw %}whether she was white or black. Bob said he had a pretty good idea but he{% endraw %}
{% raw %}wouldn't like to say.){% endraw %}
{% raw %}Well, there we were in the hospital. It was quite a large one, too. We found{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Miss Italy Morse and Miss Helen Robinson in training as nurses, for they desired{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to do their bit. They informed us that a tramp steamer had been damaged, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}amongst the hands on board were Steve Baldwin, C. R. Munford, Reed, Rainer,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}J. K. Levie, Cheney, K. C. Maddox, Bill Hogan (fireman), Carr, G. 0. Wheless,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}C. A. Johnson. Of course we were a little surprised, but we found that they{% endraw %}
{% raw %}wanted to go to France and thought that the best way. As it was, they landed in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the hospital.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}A doctor came to examine us, and finding nothing the matter but a severe{% endraw %}
{% raw %}shock, decided we had better rest for a few days.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Connected with the hospital was a farm, from which it supported itself. We{% endraw %}
{% raw %}were taken for an outing to see the farm. There we found G. B. Head and Sam{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Easley digging potatoes. Donald White was taking a litter of pigs for a walk;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Frank Wesley was feeding the stock; H. C. Bugg was cutting corn; Link was milk¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ing the cows, and Burger and Needham were shucking corn. Kurfees was walk¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ing around like he owned the place, but we found much to our sorrow that he was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}slightly "off," and they had only slight hopes of his recovery. Folsom was super¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}intendent and a good one.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}They dismissed us in due time, but liking Savannah pretty well, we stayed{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a few days. We dropped in a movie to see Beverly Bayne. Who should be her{% endraw %}
{% raw %}leading man but V. J. Altee. We always knew that he would be a leading movie{% endraw %}
{% raw %}actor. He was assisted by a most notable cast, including the now famous names{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of such men as Irvin Futch, G. F. Altee, P. C. Blount, and H. A. Benton.{% endraw %}
