{% raw %}HUDDIE LEE CHENEY{% endraw %}
{% raw %}¥ Q{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Shellman, Ga.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Cheney came to us in '15 from Shellman, Ga., af¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ter being graduated from the High Schools of that{% endraw %}
{% raw %}place. He has the very admirable trait of speaking{% endraw %}
{% raw %}when speech is necessary and remaining quiet at the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}proper time. His work has always shown the care and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}thought of the maker, and his theoretical knowledge{% endraw %}
{% raw %}goes without question. He has been one of the most{% endraw %}
{% raw %}regular attendants on lectures throughout his entire Col¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lege course and his notes make an accurate guide to the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lectures on any date. We know that his will be a large{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and remunerative practice as well as a great benefit to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the people with whom he comes in contact.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ARTHUR HAMPTON COKER{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Brewton, Ala.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Arthur came to us from Alabama. He received his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}early training there in the graded and high school. In{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Fall of '15 Arthur heard the call of Dentistry and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}quickly responded. Being one of the hardest workers{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in the class, he has accomplished many things during his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}stay with us, and has made many steadfast friends{% endraw %}
{% raw %}among the boys, who will always remember him as a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}good friend. With his increasing knowledge we predict{% endraw %}
{% raw %}for this fellow a successful career.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}¥ Q{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Greenwood, S. C.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}When the boys of '15 met Brooks for the first time,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}they met one of the most conscientious fellows they had{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ever known. Brooks received his early education in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}graded and high school of Greenwood and the Bailey{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Military Institute. Soon tiring of military life Brooks{% endraw %}
{% raw %}left school and tried his hand at mechanics in which he{% endraw %}
{% raw %}succeeded, but after staying in his father's office for a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}while he soon realized that he was to follow in his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}father's footsteps. He is possessed with a good dispo¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sition and has made numberless friends while at Col¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lege. We predict for this young man a promising{% endraw %}
{% raw %}future.{% endraw %}
