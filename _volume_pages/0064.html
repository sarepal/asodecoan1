{% raw %}ALVIS LEIGHTON WETSEL{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Big Springs, Texas{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Wetsel hails from the land of the prairie-dog and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cactus. In him we have one of the hardest workers in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}College and an excellent operator, especially in amalgam.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He is sure to reap success in the practice of his profes¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sion. He has the distinction of having passed the Texas{% endraw %}
{% raw %}State Board after his Junior year, an achievement not{% endraw %}
{% raw %}accomplished by many.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}G. OWEN WHELESS{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Nashville, Ga.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Wheless was born in Nashville, Ga., and received{% endraw %}
{% raw %}his early education there. Later he graduated from{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Moultrie Business College. After being in business{% endraw %}
{% raw %}for a few years he entered the A. D. C. in '15. He is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a young gentleman of splendid character, has won lots{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of friends, and his class-mates are assured from the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}quality of his College work that his will be a life of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}usefulness and prominence.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}DONALD WHITE{% endraw %}
{% raw %}E V *{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Atlanta, Ga.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}White was born in the little town of Austell, Ga.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He received his boyhood education in the Atlanta schools{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and has also studied business methods, commercial law{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and book-keeping. At one time he was cashier of the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}passenger department of the Chicago, Burlington and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Quincy Railroad at Omaha, Neb. He is a very ambi¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tious chap and entered the old S. D. C. with determin¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ation written all about him. White has been a good{% endraw %}
{% raw %}student, is a quiet fellow and has good reason for{% endraw %}
{% raw %}speaking every time he does.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Secretary Editorial Staff Annual 1917-'18.{% endraw %}
