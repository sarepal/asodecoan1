{% raw %}The Chemistry of Girls{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Definition: Girls are a polymeric, allotropic class of substance.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}History : The first girl was discovered in a garden under a leaf, the same{% endraw %}
{% raw %}year apples were discovered. Like the man who first made arsine, the discoverer{% endraw %}
{% raw %}forfeited his life.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}OccuiiiiENCE: Free, and in combination with men.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Physical Properties: Like crystals, girls may be distinguished from one{% endraw %}
{% raw %}another by their color, odor, density and form. They have the power of changing{% endraw %}
{% raw %}their form and color. Once or twice in a life-time you run across one altogether{% endraw %}
{% raw %}different from all the rest, though observers do not seem always to be able to see{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the great difference you observe so easily.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Chemical Properties:{% endraw %}
{% raw %}"Sugar and spice.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}And everything nice;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}That's what little girls are made of."{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The foregoing is not chemically true. Neither is it true that they are made{% endraw %}
{% raw %}up (though many are), made up, I say, altogether of dust. On the contrary,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}water forms seventy-five per cent, of their composition. Just think of it! Seven¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ty-five per cent, water, plus dust, equals girls! Can it be that these lovely crea¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tures, with the funny hats and the high-heeled oxfords, are, after all, only so many{% endraw %}
{% raw %}solidified puddles, so to speak, as it were? Perish the thought.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Truly there is more to girls than just water and dust. After years of study{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and experiment, I have succeeded in isolating the following elements, which are{% endraw %}
{% raw %}quite constant in their occurrence in these wonderful bodies: Laughs, cries, smiles,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}frowns, loves, hates, dates, kisses, deceit and sweetness.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I cannot give the technique employed to reveal all the foregoing elements.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}However, I will take the time and give in detail the procedure for obtaining{% endraw %}
{% raw %}kisses.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Experiment : The partial absence of light is desirable. Choose a girl whose{% endraw %}
{% raw %}nose turns up. This is important; you do not want a nose in your way just at the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}critical moment. Place said girl on a divan and get thereon yourself. Apply hot{% endraw %}
{% raw %}air. Snuggle. And kisses will begin to ooze to her lips, from where they may be{% endraw %}
{% raw %}easily removed.{% endraw %}
