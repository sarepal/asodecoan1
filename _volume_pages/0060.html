{% raw %}DREW IRVIN STALLINGS{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Bunn, N. C.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Stallings is one of our hardest workers. Always has{% endraw %}
{% raw %}he been earnest and honest in his efforts. A splendid{% endraw %}
{% raw %}description of him is:{% endraw %}
{% raw %}A boat may move on grandly{% endraw %}
{% raw %}While going down the stream,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}With the aid of trifling muscle{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Or only moderate steam;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}But give me the up-bound boatman,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Who dares oppose the stream,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}And throws in tougher muscle,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Or raises better steam;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Even dead fish and dry leaves,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}May with the current go;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}But all who choose the upward route,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Must nerve and courage show.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}BERLIN CLAUDE THOMASSON{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Bryson City, N. C.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Thomasson hails from Bryson City in the Tar-heel{% endraw %}
{% raw %}State. He was born in that section known as the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'' Land of the Sky'' and we are quite sure that the alti¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tude in which he was born had something to do in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}making up of this lofty, high minded young gentleman,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}whose sterling qualities have placed him in a place of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}esteem to all with whom he comes in contact. He re¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ceived his literary education in the Bryson City High{% endraw %}
{% raw %}School, and from there entered the S. D. C. in '15.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Those who know him best say that he can make a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}success anywhere.{% endraw %}
