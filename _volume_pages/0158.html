{% raw %}A View of College Life{% endraw %}
{% raw %}We 've learned our lessons well,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}We 've cut up stiffs,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}And breathed long whiffs,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Of that infernal smell.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}We 've studied dead men 's bones,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}And heard the live men 's groans,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}We 've worked hard at night,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}To learn our stuff just right.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Soon Spring will bring exams,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}With all their days and nights of crams,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}We worry and sweat, and fume and fret,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}To get them off our hands.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}When Summer comes at last,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}And D.D.S. 's we are classed,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}We give a cheer that all may hear,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Our College life is passed.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}II. W. C.{% endraw %}
