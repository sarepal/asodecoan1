{% raw %}POSITION, ETC., AT THE TABLE{% endraw %}
{% raw %}First, in this connection, should be mentioned the feet. This may seem foreign{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to the subject, but it must be remembered that your fellow guests at a dinner are{% endraw %}
{% raw %}very much interested in the location of your feet. The old-fashioned idea that{% endraw %}
{% raw %}they should be on the floor has been exploded, as they should, in reality, be most{% endraw %}
{% raw %}anywhere but there. The most popular place at present seems to be on the feet of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}your neighbor, provided you are a gentleman and your neighbors are ladies, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}that 3-our neighbors are wearing white shoes and stockings. This should be ascer¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tained immediately upon sitting down by crawling under the table. If, upon thor¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ough examination, it is found impossible to use your immediate neighbors as repos¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}itories for the feet, an effort should be made to place them in the lap of the party{% endraw %}
{% raw %}immediately opposite to you. LTnfortunately some people are prohibited by nature{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from doing this, in which event, and as a last resort, the feet should be wound{% endraw %}
{% raw %}carefully around the legs of your chair where the butler will be sure to stumble{% endraw %}
{% raw %}over them.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Next in importance is the napkin. The use of this article is steadily growing{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in popularity. In fact, it is now being almost generally used, but the author has{% endraw %}
{% raw %}observed that many people do not seem to realize exactly what it is for.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Some simply spread it across the knees, but this is a gross breach of form.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The proper place for it, on a. male guest, is around the edge of the collar, provided,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of course, the guest is wearing a collar. If not, it should be tucked into the arm-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}holes of the vest. In case the guest is not wearing a vest; which of course he{% endraw %}
{% raw %}should at a formal affair, the napkin should be tied around the neck, the ends{% endraw %}
{% raw %}gracefully draped over the chest. It will be found that used in this way, it will{% endraw %}
{% raw %}serve as an excellent receptacle for the soup which escapes in the function that{% endraw %}
{% raw %}follows.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}One of the most important reasons for the napkin being placed around the neck{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is to prevent the guests from using it to wipe the perspiration from the forehead{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and behind the ears. In good society, this is not being done at present.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}GRAPE FRUIT{% endraw %}
{% raw %}A great many dinners are being started with this fruit at present, and as the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}average diner has great difficulty in handling it, a few words on the subject will{% endraw %}
{% raw %}probablv not be out of place. Most hosts and hostesses are now furnishing a little{% endraw %}
{% raw %}instrument known as a grape fruit compass to each guest. The fruit should then{% endraw %}
{% raw %}be placed firmly on the floor, the diner kneeling with one knee on each side of it.{% endraw %}
