{% raw %}The Atlanta-Southern Dental College{% endraw %}
{% raw %}MIE Atlanta-Southern Dental College is a combination of words which, to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the casual observer, means but little, but to the dental profession and peo¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ple of the South, the full understanding of its true meaning brings a thrill{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of gratification and a deep sense of satisfaction. To the dental profession, be¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cause a combination of the two leading institutions for the teaching of dentistry{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in the South, means that petty jealousies, rivalry and competition are eliminated{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and that the ambition for a college that could more nearly realize the "ideal" is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}made possible.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}With the faculty of the two former colleges, the dental and medical profession{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of Atlanta, to draw from, a combination of teachers is available which may easily{% endraw %}
{% raw %}be classed among the peers of faculties for professional schools.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The equipment selected from the two former colleges has been supplemented{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in the various departments and an entirely new department of laboratory physi¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ology has been opened which is but one of the various features which are to be{% endraw %}
{% raw %}added each year in an effort to keep abreast the wonderful progress being achieved{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in the dental science.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}^{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The joining of hands of the alumni of the two former institutions will have{% endraw %}
{% raw %}its effect over the entire South, as it will harmonize their efforts in their various{% endraw %}
{% raw %}localities for the uplift of their chosen profession.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}To the people of the South it means a better educated dentist, both from the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}literary and professional standpoint, to serve them.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}It makes possible higher preliminary educational requirements for entrance,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a more thoroughly trained and more competent dentist being graduated.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Appreciating as they do the value of dental surgery and the meaning in con¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}nection with health and longevity, much is to be gained by this combination, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}hearty approval is being daily voiced.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}We, the Class of 1918, feel that we are to be congratulated as being the first{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to receive diplomas from this institution, and express our appreciation by say¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ing that the value of the combination of Atlanta-Southern Dental College is kindly{% endraw %}
{% raw %}appreciated, thoroughly approved and heartily endorsed, and bind ourselves uncon¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ditionally to work hereafter for success so much deserved.{% endraw %}
