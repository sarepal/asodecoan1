{% raw %}JESSE ROSS TUCKER{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Conyers, Ga.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Tucker started his career in life with a position on{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a railroad. His ambitions being higher he decided to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}become a professional man, and consequently entered{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the A. D. C. in '15. Having in his possession those{% endraw %}
{% raw %}essentials which make him a leader, he has acquired{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the friendship and esteem of both the faculty and his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}class-mates. His future is bright, and we see in him{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a leader in the profession which he has chosen.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Associate Editor Annual 1918.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}MARTHA IONE TURNER{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Atlanta, Ga.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Miss Turner came to us from the clay hills of North{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Georgia. For three years she has been one of us and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}each year her circle of friends has increased. She is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}noted for her quietness of manner and her splendid at-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tentiveness to study. We wish for her much success in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the profession.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Associate Editor Annual, 1918.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}JOSEPH VINES TURNER{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Oxford, N. C.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Turner was born in Oxford, N. C. Attended the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}public schools of his locality and attended the Durham{% endraw %}
{% raw %}High School. He has quite a varied career, having{% endraw %}
{% raw %}taught school, traveled a bit, done some office work, as{% endraw %}
{% raw %}well as being in the employment of one of our rail¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}roads, and last and greatest he married—when he de¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cided to settle down. Turner is one of those deep stu¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}dents who are not satisfied with learning the mere super¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ficialities of a subject, but delve deeper, simply because{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of a desire to acquire a well founded education. He is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}one of the best of our operators as well as being a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}splendid theorist.{% endraw %}
