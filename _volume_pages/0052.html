{% raw %}ALEXANDER NEWTON{% endraw %}
{% raw %}¥ Q{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Casar, N. C.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Newton saw the first brilliant North Carolina sunrise{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in the quiet dreamy village of Casar. Being a young{% endraw %}
{% raw %}man of high ambition and not exactly content on the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}farm he decided to enter the professional world and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}made up his mind to become a D.D.S. Having secured{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a splendid foundational education at Wake Forest Col¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lege, he came to us in October, '15. Since that time{% endraw %}
{% raw %}he has been a diligent and persevering student. He is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}one of the most popular members of the Class by reason{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of the fact of his dry humor and readiness with which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}he loans his belongings.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}JOHN HARVEY NICHOLSON{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Houstonville, N. C.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}This young man came to us from Houstonville, N. C,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in '15, and is a splendid example of the manhood of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}that great State. He is noted among the ladies for his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}good looks and charming manners and among his class¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mates by his readiness to lend anything he possesses.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}His good nature has gained him the friendship of all{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with whom he comes in contact. Since entering Col¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lege his life has been characterized by those high moral{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and social principles which are incorporated in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}make-up of every true gentleman. He has made excel¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lent records in theory and his practical work.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}WILLIAM GEORGE NIMOCKS{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Fayetteville, N. C.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}"Little in stature but big in heart." This little{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fellow came to us in the Fall of '15. His three years{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in College have been well spent, and we who know him{% endraw %}
{% raw %}best feel certain that his success is assured. Nimocks is{% endraw %}
{% raw %}known to us as "Nimmy," "Nimrod," or "Bill"—{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which vouches for his popularity.{% endraw %}
