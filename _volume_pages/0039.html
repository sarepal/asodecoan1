{% raw %}BERNET FRIEDMAN{% endraw %}
{% raw %}EM'*{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Brooklyn, N. Y.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He comes to us from the hustling City of Brooklyn,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}N. Y., and received his literary education in that place;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}after completing his education he was engaged for some{% endraw %}
{% raw %}time in the drug business as a pharmacist, but deciding{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to study Dentistry he came southward and stopped in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Atlanta, where he entered the S. D. C. Since he has{% endraw %}
{% raw %}been with us he has made a number of friends by his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}frank and open manner; he is always ready to help a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fellow student in any way he can. He is a hard worker{% endraw %}
{% raw %}both in theory and practical work. We think he will{% endraw %}
{% raw %}achieve a well merited success.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}IRVIN SMITH FUTCH{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Alachua, Fla.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Irvin, a fair-haired, gray-eyed Georgian, was born in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Quitman, Ga., but early in life moved to the '' Land of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Flowers'' where he received his early education in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Alachua High School from which he graduated in '14.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He was inclined to- be a farmer and merchant but a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}continual reminder of Dentistry gave him the idea of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}spending one year in the University in preparing for Den¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tistry. He arrived at the S. D. C. in October, '15. He{% endraw %}
{% raw %}expects to return to the '' Land of Flowers'' on com¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}pletion of his course.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}STEVE ARTHUR GARRETT{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Austell, Ga.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'' Steve,'' after having spent two years at Emory{% endraw %}
{% raw %}College, preparatory to the study of Dentistry, entered{% endraw %}
{% raw %}A. D. 0. in '15. Since that time he has been a leader{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in his class and has been noted for the earnest perform¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ance of every duty that has been placed in his charge.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}We predict great success for "Steve".{% endraw %}
