{% raw %}Senior Class History{% endraw %}
{% raw %}DURING the Fall of 1915 "Dr. Shep" and "Dr. Claude" spent much of their{% endraw %}
{% raw %}valuable time corresponding with boys whose names they could get ranging{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from nineteen to thirty-one years old. They used such high sounding{% endraw %}
{% raw %}phrases that they thoroughly succeeded in convincing the young men that life was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}not worth living without the degree of D.D.S.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}On October 1st, 1915, about 150 boys and girls from every walk of life came{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to Atlanta, each having a great ambition to be a dental surgeon. On entering the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Dean's Office we were greeted with a smile and a "Glad to see you Mr. ----, have{% endraw %}
{% raw %}a seat." After extracting $200.00 of our hard-earned money we left the office{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with a tap on the back and these words, "All right. Doctor, call again."{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Life became different, at least with the boys. Two "Saturday nights" each{% endraw %}
{% raw %}week, a shave every other day and a tonic twice a week on our "Charlie Chaplin"{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mustache, a seat each week in the "peanut" gallery of a vaudeville show, was our{% endraw %}
{% raw %}procedure for the first year. We were strictly Freshmen.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Our professors were "mighty big-" men in those days, and how we would{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tremble when they would utter such phrases as "Squamous epithelium," "Strep¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tococcus viridans." "Levator labii superioris alaeque nasi." To all this we became{% endraw %}
{% raw %}accustomed and side-tracked our discussion to "Bush-whacking. "State Board,"{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and "Locating in some big city."{% endraw %}
{% raw %}After the usual joys of a Summer vacation, once more we find ourselves in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Atlanta. At this time we begin carving on the human cadaver (which means{% endraw %}
{% raw %}elimination of hash from the menu). Now, to find the gastrocnemius muscle,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}locate the solar plexus, learn how to bound the quadrilateral triangle, trace the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}blood from incisor to great phalanges, learn the function of the various muscles:{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Orbicularis oris, orbicularis palpebrarum, corrugator supercilli, compressor narium{% endraw %}
{% raw %}minor. After digesting all of this we realized that superior maxillary only meant{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the upper jaw, and that stomatitis didn't mean inflammation of the stomach.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Now, I pray and beseech you that you don't breathe through the oesophagus, nor{% endraw %}
{% raw %}swallow through the foramen magnum; and ye gods! learn how to spell symphysis,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and don't put two s's in "pus." Young gentlemen. I would have you to learn{% endraw %}
{% raw %}that sodium and potassium carbonates increase the viscosity of the blood. We{% endraw %}
{% raw %}have long since learned to recognize the familiar words in pathology lecture,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}"Where are you my son?" and the reply to the answer. "Thank you, boy." In{% endraw %}
{% raw %}radiology it is "a-b-s-o-l-u-t-e-l-y" essential to know how to adjust the X-ray tube.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Much of our valuable time was required in securing necessary skill, proper{% endraw %}
{% raw %}technique, and learning how to properly distribute heat; and for a little real taste{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of life's work we made crowns of all descriptions, using a piece of solder the size{% endraw %}
