{% raw %}Leaving the next morning we arrived in Atlanta that evening. Not being{% endraw %}
{% raw %}very tired and fond of dancing, we dropped in Lane's Dance Hall. We tried to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}get by the ticket man. but were stopped by him. We found him to he none other{% endraw %}
{% raw %}than our old chum, Frank Lentz. lie had been at it since graduating, and had{% endraw %}
{% raw %}made a success apparently.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}There were quite a few soldiers up there and among them were Ilartslield,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Bussell (now grey-headed), Callahan. Gilliland, II. I-]. Wood. Weatherman. C. 0.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Wells (playing a bass drum in orchestra), Rubin, M. M. Parks, .1. II. Nicholson,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Hunter (singing Dutch songs in Italian), and several other novelties, hut being{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tired we departed.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Having thanked our kind friend for his guidance and companionship, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}rewarded him for his time, we separated, each unto his own way, and the Prophet{% endraw %}
{% raw %}still lives.{% endraw %}
