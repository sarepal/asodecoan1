{% raw %}CURTIS ELREE RUTLEDGE{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Cheneyville, La.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Rutledge was born in Cheneyville, La., and received{% endraw %}
{% raw %}his early education in the grammar and high schools of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}his community; after which he attended the Transyl¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}vania University, Lexington, Ky., for two years, coming{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from there to study Dentistry in the A. D. C, in '15.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He has endeared himself to his school-mates by his ever{% endraw %}
{% raw %}present smile, sunny disposition, and staunch loyalty to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}his friends. During his College career he has demon¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}strated unusual ability both in theory and practice{% endraw %}
{% raw %}thereby winning for himself an enviable position in his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}class-standing.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}JOHN THOMAS RUTLEDGE{% endraw %}
{% raw %}E <F ${% endraw %}
{% raw %}Pageland, S. C.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Rutledge is another one of the '' Sand Hill'' boys,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}who found his way into the profession through the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}gates of the old S. D. C. He entered school in October,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}'15, after finishing his preliminary education in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Pageland High School. Rutledge is well known by his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fellow students, and has made many valuable friend¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ships while in College. He is noted for his regular at¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tendance to lectures, and we feel sure that he will be{% endraw %}
{% raw %}equally as attentive to his practice.{% endraw %}
