{% raw %}tween us with saw dust all over him. His beard \vras so heavy you couldn't tell{% endraw %}

{% raw %}We said good-bye and resumed our flying trip and looking around through my{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bottle I saw far away the town of Hickory. It was just growing dark and suddenly{% endraw %}
{% raw %}there flashed a big electric sign, "Yount & Campbell, Painless Dentists." I was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}somewhat surprised, but my companions assured me that they would soon find the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}error of their ways and return to the fold.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I tried hard to see more by night but the fog was thick, so landing seemed{% endraw %}
{% raw %}advisable. We saw the lights of Waynesville, N. C, and "dropped in." We found{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the town somewhat astir and decided to investigate. On entering a large wooden{% endraw %}
{% raw %}structure we were handed a program headed "Farmers' Convention." Feeling at{% endraw %}
{% raw %}home we decided to stay. I read my program and whose name should I see but{% endraw %}
{% raw %}some of my former classmates. N. M. Medford being chairman pro tern, called the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}meeting to order. The opening address was given by M. J. Doub, of East Bend, on{% endraw %}
{% raw %}"The Proper Spreading of Fertilizer" (Bull). Next on the program was a solo by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Alex Newton, who was ably assisted at the piano by Miss lone Turner. Among{% endraw %}
{% raw %}other speakers of note were Thrailkill and C. H. Bryan, of Apex, N. C, E. E. Har¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mon, Jr., B. C. Thomasson and E. D. Jones. Being a little hesitant about leaving,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}as it was raining, we were politely informed by J. L. West and L. J. Dupree that{% endraw %}
{% raw %}as all the lamps had to be blown out we would have to leave.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Wishing to keep as dry as possible, we stepped out on the sidewalk and saw a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}movie across the street. We went in and found to our amazement that other friends{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of ours were there, as there stood before us two perfectly groomed ushers in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}form of Poindexter and F. G. Johnson. We learned that a special program had been{% endraw %}
{% raw %}put on that day for the benefit of the "Convention." Much to our delight, our{% endraw %}
{% raw %}friend. Bill Nimocks, stepped out and sang "The Lost Chord"—a voice of rather{% endraw %}
{% raw %}pleasing quality, but not the same voice he had while in College. I was sorry for{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I was sure that dissipation was the cause.{% endraw %}
