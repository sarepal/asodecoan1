{% raw %}ARTHUR FINLEY LINK{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Atlanta, Ga.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}After receiving his preliminary education in the pub¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lic schools of Atlanta, and later spending a few years{% endraw %}
{% raw %}at Gordon Institute, Peacock School for Boys, and At¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}lanta School of Pharmacy, he decided to follow in his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}father's footsteps and therefore entered the S. D. C. in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the Fall of '15. He is a man of high ideals, sincere{% endraw %}
{% raw %}spirit, a good student, and will make a Dentist Atlanta{% endraw %}
{% raw %}should be proud of. He is not much of a talker, but{% endraw %}
{% raw %}possesses a rarer and much better quality as a good{% endraw %}
{% raw %}listener.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}QUINCE GLADSTONE LOGAN{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Rutherfordton, N. C.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Logan entered our midst in the Fall of '15, after{% endraw %}
{% raw %}completing his literary education at Mar's Hill and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Valparaiso University. He was drafted into the army{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in the Fall of '17, but later was released from service to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}finish his dental education. Logan has made a good{% endraw %}
{% raw %}student and a hard worker, and is highly esteemed by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}his class-mates. We predict for him a future success.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}KLEBER C. MADDOX{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Marietta, Ga.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Maddox is an industrious young fellow who for the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}last three years has arisen at 5 a. m. in order to reach{% endraw %}
{% raw %}College in time to see the doors of the lecture hall close.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}We think that Uncle Sam needs an army of men with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}such grit to send against the Kaiser. During these three{% endraw %}
{% raw %}years, in his quiet kind of way, he has labored diligently,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and has always made good in his work. We feel sure{% endraw %}
{% raw %}that he will be equally as successful in the practice of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}his profession.{% endraw %}
