{% raw %}The Better Thing{% endraw %}
{% raw %}It is better to lose with a conscience clean{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Than to win by a trick unfair;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}It is better to fail and know you 've been,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Whatever the prize was, square,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Than to claim the joy of a far off goal{% endraw %}
{% raw %}And the cheers of the standers-by,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}And to know deep down in your inmost soul{% endraw %}
{% raw %}A cheat you must live and die.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Who wins by trick may take the prize{% endraw %}
{% raw %}And at first he may think it sweet,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}But many a day in the future lies{% endraw %}
{% raw %}When he '11 wish he had met defeat.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}For the man who lost will be glad at heart{% endraw %}
{% raw %}And walk with his head up high,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}While his conqueror knows he must play the par{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Of a cheat and a living lie.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The prize seems fair when the fight is on,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}But save it is truly won{% endraw %}
{% raw %}You will hate the thing when the crowds are gone{% endraw %}
{% raw %}For it stands for a false deed done.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}And it's better you should never reach your goal{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Than ever success to buy{% endraw %}
{% raw %}At the price of knowing down deep in your soul{% endraw %}
{% raw %}That your glory is all a lie.{% endraw %}
